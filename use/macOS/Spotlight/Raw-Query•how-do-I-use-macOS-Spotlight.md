---
title: How do I use macOS Spotlight's Raw Data syntax?
---

# To enable
- Finder
- ⌘+⌥+[SPACE]
- "+" (next to Save)
- drop-down on "Name", choose "Other…"
- search "Raw query"
- enable checkbox
- "OK"
